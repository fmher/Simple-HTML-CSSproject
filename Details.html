Details of the Party

Welcome to my birthday bash! Here's everything you need to know about what to expect on the big day:

When and Where?

  • Date: 12/14
  • Time: 12:00 PM - 9:00 PM
  • Location: My House

Costume Party Rules

The theme is costume party, so bring your most creative, funniest, or coolest costume! If you don’t wear a costume, you’ll face the ultimate penalty—everyone at the party will get to paint your face however they like. Don’t worry, it’s all in good fun!

We might even host a costume contest with prizes for the best costumes, so be ready to stand out!

Activities and Fun

  • Photo Booth: Capture your best moments in themed backdrops with fun props.
  • Games and Challenges: Participate in exciting games and challenges.
  • DIY Face Painting: Get creative with painting your friends' faces or have yours painted.
  • Music and Dancing: Show off your moves to some great tunes.

Food and Refreshments

Delicious snacks, finger foods, and beverages will be available throughout the party. The highlight of the day is cake time at 2:00 PM, so don’t miss it!

Gifts (Optional)

While your presence is the greatest gift, feel free to bring something if you'd like!
